💡 Key Insights & Recommendations
Prioritized actions based on your data. Jun–Aug 2026 is your make-or-break window.
🔴
73% promo dependency High Priority
6.4M UZS in discounts vs 1.31M revenue. Now that validation is done, reduce to <40% by Q3 2026. Even dropping to 50% roughly doubles your monthly revenue at the same order volume.
🔴
Account capacity risk High Priority
blazerent1 alone handles 17% of all sessions. At 6-club scale (252 sessions/month), you need ~30 accounts but only have 10. Add ~20 more before clubs generate real volume or you'll hit bottlenecks and ban risk.
🟡
108 never-ordered users Medium Priority
These users registered but never ordered. They have intent (signed up). One targeted message — maybe a promo — could convert 20–40 of them. At 2.65 avg orders, that's potentially 200–400K UZS in LTV.
🟡
Investigate March 2026 spike Medium Priority
March had 76 new signups — nearly double any other month. What caused it? If you can identify and repeat it, that's your growth playbook. Could be club promotions, a specific game launch, or word-of-mouth cascade.
🟡
Card payments only 25% Medium Priority
75% of customers pay via balance/promo credits. This limits cash flow predictability. As promos reduce, push card payment adoption — it creates real cash flow and makes revenue projections reliable.
🔴
Plaintext passwords in spreadsheet Security Risk
Several customer records store passwords in plaintext (e.g. "123", "kaylo", "qwerty"). If the sheet leaks, those accounts are exposed. Hash all passwords server-side (bcrypt) and remove plaintext from this sheet immediately.
🟢
15× revenue growth with zero ad spend Positive Signal
From 27.5K (Nov) to 405K (May) using only word-of-mouth and 2 clubs. This organic growth rate is extremely strong for a gaming SaaS. The product clearly has demand in this market.
🟢
67% activation rate Positive Signal
Industry avg for zero-investment onboarding is 40–60%. At 67% you're above average, suggesting the product is intuitive enough that most users who find it figure it out and try it.
🔵
Jun–Aug 2026 is your decision window Watch Closely
You now have 6 clubs (3× distribution) and are reducing promos. If Jun–Aug shows 200+ orders/month and 40%+ paid rate, you have a scalable business. If not, you'll have clean data to pivot from.
🔵
Top 10 users = 34% of orders Concentration Risk
Shaxram (38 orders, 0 UZS paid) and Амир (51 orders, 30K UZS) drive massive volume. These are likely club regulars. Protect these relationships but don't depend on them — broaden the active base through the new clubs.
Business Verdict
Continue — June to August is the proof window
The organic growth signal is strong enough to justify continuing. No other product achieves 15× revenue growth in 6 months from 2 clubs and word of mouth. The next 60–90 days with 6 clubs and reduced promos will give you the real unit economics.
